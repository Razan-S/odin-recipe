Beef Ragu

The Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (2 pound) boneless beef chuck roast, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons harissa (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • bay leaf
  • 1 pound pappardelle pasta, cooked and drained
  • grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

The way to make the dish

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high.
  2. Season chuck roast with pepper and 2 teaspoons salt.
  3. Sear meat until browned on every sides
  4. Remove from Dutch oven and transfer to a large plate.
  5. Reduce heat to medium and stir in onion. Cook, until softened
  6. Add garlic and cook
  7. Pour in wine and cook, scraping the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon to loosen any browned bits, until reduced by two thirds
  8. Stir in tomatoes, bay leaf, chuck roast, and remaining 1 teaspoon salt
  9. Reduce heat to medium-low and cover. Cook, stirring occasionally, until meat is tender, sauce has thickened, and flavors meld, about 2 1/2 hours.
  10. Remove and discard bay leaf. Transfer meat from Dutch oven to a cutting board and shred using 2 forks. Return meat to pot and stir in pappardelle. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
