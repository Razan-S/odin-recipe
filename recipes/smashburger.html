Description

a burger thatâ€™s made by smashing two thin beef patties together with cheese in the middle.

The Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Butter
  • Ground chuck beef
  • squares parchment paper
  • salt
  • American cheese

The way to make

  1. Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast on the flat-top until lightly browned. Set aside.
  2. Form meat loosely-packed balls
  3. Place each ball on the hot flat-top, cover with a piece of parchment
  4. immediately smash down to a 1/4 inch thickness using 2 stiff, sturdy spatulas that are criss-crossed to get proper leverage as you press down.
  5. Grill for about 45 seconds, until the edges are dark brown and the centers are a light pink color.
  6. Grill an additional 15 to 20 seconds; stack the plain patties over the cheese-covered patties
